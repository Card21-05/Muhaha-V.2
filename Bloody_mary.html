Bloody Mary

This classic cocktail combines vodka, tomato juice, and spices.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz vodka
  • 4 oz tomato juice
  • 1/2 oz lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • Tabasco sauce to taste
  • Celery stick and lime wedge (for garnish)
